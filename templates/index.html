CUT · WK 1-8

Aggressive Cut Training

VOLUME10-16 sets/muscle/week
INTENSITYRPE 7-8
CARDIOLISS 3-4x/wk 30-40min
STEPS8,000-10,000 daily
DELOADEvery 4th week -40% volume
Recovery is limited on a deficit. Drop the last set on isolation exercises when fatigue is high — compounds are priority. Refeed every 10-14 days.
STRENGTH · WK 11-17

Strength Block

VOLUME8-12 sets/muscle/week
INTENSITYRPE 8-9 Heavier loads
REP RANGESCompounds 4x4-6
CARDIOLISS 2-3x/wk 30 min
DELOADWK 14 — deload week (-40% vol)
FOCUSBench, Squat, Deadlift, OHP
Lower volume, higher intensity. Strength gains here are mostly neurological. Deload at WK 14 to prevent CNS burnout.
PEAK HYPERTROPHY · WK 18-24

Slow Cut Volume Phase

VOLUME14-20 sets/muscle/week
INTENSITYRPE 7-8 Pump-style
TECHNIQUESDrop sets, myo-reps, pre-exhaust
CARDIOLISS 3-4x/wk 30-40 min
DELOADWK 20 — deload week (-40% vol)
REFEEDSBi-weekly then weekly
Most visible transformation phase. Push volume and metabolic stress. Deload at WK 20 — your body NEEDS it after 10 weeks of cutting.

// Supplement stack
MUST HAVE
Creatine Monohydrate
5g/day · any time · with water
Replenishes ATP. Strength, volume, lean mass retention. Most studied supplement ever.
MUST HAVE
Whey Protein
1-2 scoops/day · post-workout
Hits 210g+ protein when food alone falls short.
MUST HAVE
Vitamin D3 + K2
2,000-5,000 IU D3 · 100mcg K2
Essential for testosterone, immunity, mood. Get tested.
RECOMMENDED
Omega-3 (Fish Oil)
2-3g EPA+DHA/day · with meals
Anti-inflammatory, joint health, body composition.
RECOMMENDED
Caffeine / Black Coffee
100-200mg pre-workout · before 1pm
Performance, focus, mild fat oxidation. Cap at 400mg/day.
MUST HAVE
Magnesium Glycinate
400mg before bed · daily
Improves sleep quality, reduces muscle cramps, supports recovery and testosterone. Glycinate form is best absorbed and least likely to cause GI issues.
RECOMMENDED
Electrolytes (Na + K + Mg)
Training days · intra-workout or AM
Critical during cut phases when water/sodium flush is high. Prevents cramps, brain fog, and performance drops. Use sugar-free electrolyte mix or LMNT.
// Rules that matter
+ DO
  • Track calories with an app
  • Hit protein target every single day
  • Progressive overload even in a cut
  • Walk 8,000-10,000 steps daily
  • Weigh daily, track weekly average
  • Monthly progress photos
  • Sleep 7-9 hours non-negotiable
  • Hit 30-40g fiber daily (veggies, oats, berries)
  • Drink 3.5-4L water daily (more on training days — also flushes uric acid)
  • Carb cycle: +30g carbs on training days, -30g on rest days
  • Stay patient - 32 weeks transforms physique completely
x DO NOT
  • Crash diet below 1,800 kcal
  • Do HIIT during cut phases — save conditioning for bulk phase (WK 25+) when recovery supports it
  • Skip lifting if tired - reduced beats none
  • Cheat meals on weekends
  • Weigh after meals or post-training
  • Cut carbs to zero
  • Skip deload weeks (WK 14 + WK 20 are mandatory)
  • Push max volume during deep deficit — drop last set of isolations when fatigued
  • Eat high-purine foods in large amounts (red meat, organ meat, shellfish, sardines, anchovies) — triggers uric acid flare-ups
  • Panic when scale stalls 1-2 weeks
